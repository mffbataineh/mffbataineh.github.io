üìå Important Notes

UAEU Pure Portal: Pure is UAEU's official Research Information Management System where all faculty research profiles, publications, projects, and collaborations are showcased. Your publications are automatically imported from major databases like Scopus and Web of Science. Visit the Pure Portal to explore research happening at UAEU and connect with colleagues.

For UAEU Students: Most paid resources like IEEE Xplore, Scopus, Web of Science, and ScienceDirect are available free through the university library. Use your UAEU credentials to access them.

Software Licenses: MATLAB and other engineering software may be available through university licenses. Check with the IT department or your college for campus agreements.

Need Help? If you can't access any of these resources or need guidance on using them, feel free to reach out during office hours or via email.
