Opportunities for Prospective Students

Join My Research Group!

  • Undergraduate Research (SURE Plus): Apply for funded research grants (40,000 AED) to work on cutting-edge projects in machine learning, computer vision, and assistive technologies
  • Senior Graduation Projects (GP I & GP II): Multiple project opportunities available in wireless communications, IoT, AI applications, and computational biology
  • Master's Programs: Accepting Master's students interested in communications theory, signal processing, and interdisciplinary research
  • PhD Opportunities: Limited positions available for highly motivated PhD candidates with strong backgrounds in electrical engineering, communications, or computer science
  • Academic Advising: Open to advising motivated students in electrical engineering and communications programs
  • Research Assistantships: Opportunities may be available through active research grants for qualified graduate students

What I Look For in Students

  • ▸ Strong foundation in mathematics, programming, and electrical engineering fundamentals
  • ▸ Curiosity and passion for research and learning
  • ▸ Self-motivation and ability to work independently
  • ▸ Good communication skills in English (written and verbal)
  • ▸ Programming experience in MATLAB, Python, or similar tools
  • ▸ Interest in interdisciplinary research combining multiple fields
